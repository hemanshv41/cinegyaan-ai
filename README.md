# 🎬 CineGyaanAI

CineGyaanAI is an AI-powered study platform that explains complex concepts through cinematic storytelling. Instead of boring explanations, concepts are presented like movie scenes to make learning more engaging and memorable.

## 🚀 Features

### 🎥 AI Movie Explainer
Learn difficult concepts through vivid cinematic storytelling. AI converts complex topics into engaging movie-style explanations.

### 📄 PDF Study Tool
Upload PDFs and instantly generate:
- Smart summaries
- Structured study notes
- Key concepts

### 🧠 Quiz Generator
Automatically generate quizzes from:
- Any topic
- Uploaded PDFs
- Study notes

### 🗂 Flashcards
Create AI-powered flashcards to quickly revise important concepts.

### 📅 Study Planner
Organize study sessions with a smart task and learning planner.

---

## 🎯 Goal
CineGyaanAI aims to make learning **fun, immersive, and easier to understand** by combining:
- AI
- Storytelling
- Smart study tools

Instead of memorizing, students **experience concepts like scenes in a movie.**
 Tech Vision

The platform is designed to evolve into a complete AI learning ecosystem including:
- AI concept storytelling
- Smart knowledge extraction from documents
- Automated revision systems
- Personalized study planning
